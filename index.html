ABOUT ME

At the age of 10 I went to music school for guitar. At the age of 14 I
formed a punk rock band with friends and performed as an electric
guitarist. At the age of 15 entered the Uzhhorod State Music School.
Participated in many concerts and is a winner of international guitar
competitions.

In 2015 entered the Lysenko National Academy of Music in Lviv. I have
participated in various concerts as an electro, bass and classical
guitarist. In 2016, I started taking guitar lessons. In 2018, I worked in
Shanghai in a band as an electric guitarist for one year.
